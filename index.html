A note to start

Why this site exists

Every family carries its history in scattered places — a grandmother's memory, an old photo album, a WhatsApp forward no one saved properly. This site is our attempt to put it all in one place, so a cousin ten years from now can still find out who Prabir Jethu's wife was, or when the last big reunion happened.

It's a living page. Every generation adds to it.
