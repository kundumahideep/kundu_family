A note to start

Why this register exists

A family chart once carried by hand from one gathering to the next only survives as long as someone keeps carrying it. This site is that chart, kept somewhere it can't be misplaced — twenty-two generations of recorded ancestry leading to Ch. Des Raj Kundu, and four more generations since, all in one place.

It is a living record. Every generation adds to it.
